Ajimy 54'' Long Handle Soft Bristle Broom

Designed for effortless, deep cleaning, the Ajimy 54'' Long Handle Soft Bristle Broom combines comfort with powerful performance. Whether you're sweeping your kitchen, hallway, or even outdoor spaces, this versatile tool ensures a clean that's both effective and easy on your body.

Why Choose This Broom?

  • 54-Inch Long Handle: A longer reach means you can sweep with less strain, making it perfect for taller users and hard-to-reach corners.
  • Soft Bristle Technology: The gentle yet firm bristles are ideal for delicate surfaces and effectively remove dust, crumbs, and hair from a variety of areas.
  • Adjustable Handle: Customize the sweeping height to match your needs, adding comfort and versatility to your cleaning routine.
  • Thick 6-Layer Bristles: These bristles are designed to capture fine particles and work hard to lift stubborn messes without damaging floors.
  • Multi-Use Design: The forked bristle end is especially great for kitchens, grabbing crumbs and small debris easily while leaving your floors spotless.

Perfect for Every Surface

This soft bristle broom is compatible with a wide range of flooring types, including hardwood, tile, concrete, and marble. Its adaptable design makes it suitable for home use, office maintenance, and even commercial spaces like stores and warehouses.

Uncompromising Cleaning Performance

With advanced bristle sanding technology, this long handle broom increases friction to help remove dirt and grime thoroughly. No need for harsh chemicals or extra effort, just a simple sweep and a sparkling clean result.
